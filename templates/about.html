If You're Not Growing, You're Dying.


This quote resonates deeply with me and reflects my approach in life: I am constantly seeking opportunities for growth and ways to use my personal and professional growth to contribute to others.

From teaching English and digital literacy to immigrant stay-at-home moms so that they are empowered to help their children with homework, have parent-teacher conferences, take their children to doctor appointments, and participate in the conversations independently, etc...

To teaching college and career readiness and financial and business literacy to teens...

To developing programs that empower professional women to leave work that they hate and do what they love...

One theme has been consistent throughout my work: Freedom. Freedom to look beyond your limitations and current situation and then create the life that YOU want.

Two things that I love most about Software Engineering is that I get to learn new things regularly and help build programs that make a difference in people's lives. I look forward to the day when I am able to share these technology skills to help uplift others.
